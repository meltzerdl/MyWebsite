HIV in California: A Tale of Two Cities

While HIV prevalence tends to cluster in particular areas of cities, has been studied from a number of angles, new cases are not necessarily always occurring in the same areas, as cities are constantly changing. For two major California cities, SF and LA, we see that new cases are commonly occurring outside of the areas of highest prevalence.
